適当な式
==================================================================

数式かけます. :math:`\sin\theta`, :math:`\cos\theta`, :math:`\tan\theta` .

.. math::
  :nowrap:

  \begin{eqnarray}
   L[Ri(t) + v_0(t)] &=& L[v_i(t)] \\
   RI(s) + V_0(s) &=& V_i(s)
  \end{eqnarray}

.. math::
  :nowrap:

  \begin{eqnarray}
   L[v_0(s)] &=&  L\left[\frac{1}{C} \int^t_0 i(\tau) d\tau \right] \\
   V_0(s) &=& \frac{1}{Cs}I(s) \\
   I(s) &=& CsV_0(s)
  \end{eqnarray}
